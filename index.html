Meet the Ice Queens Behind the NHL’s Biggest Stars

By Simon Saunders - July 10, 2022

This article appeared in Unpasted and has been published here with permission.

Relationships often take a lot of work to get them to a good place. This can be made more difficult when your significant other works and travels for a living. Athletes often travel around and still manage to build long and happy marriages with their wives. In this article, we take a look at the beautiful wives of some of our favorite NHL players.

Joan Vergel - Brendan Gallagher

Power couple Joan and Brendan. Even though Joan lives in a different city, long distance is not an obstacle. Brendan also does not seem to mind that Joan is not a fan of his current team, as she loves the Winnipeg Jets.

ADVERTISEMENT

They met at an autograph signing outside a hotel before the Habs had a game against the Jets. Since then they've been the NHL's most influential power couple.

ADVERTISEMENT

Despite spending most of their time in LA due to Drew's job, their Canadian roots drew them home for their wedding. Doughty credits his wife for making fatherhood feel easy and making defeats seem like wins when he comes home. The couple has two children, a three-year-old as well as a one-year-old.
